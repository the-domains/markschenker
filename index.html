Leaving Shore

Picture yourself on a creaky wooden boat. Listen to the boards strain against the water as the ocean rocks it back and forth. The boat is drifting along, and soon you find yourself alone. There is at once both a profound sense of peace in the isolation of the waves as well as a building fear: "Will I ever make it home?"

The secret of change is to focus all of your energy, not on fighting the old but on building the new.
